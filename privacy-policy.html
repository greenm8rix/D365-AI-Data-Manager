Privacy Policy

D365 AI Data Manager — Chrome Extension

We do not collect, store, or sell your data.

No servers. No analytics. No tracking. No telemetry. Your data never touches our infrastructure.

How It Works

The extension runs entirely in your browser. OData queries go directly from your browser to your D365 environment using your existing session. No intermediary servers are involved.

Preferences (favorites, saved queries, settings) are saved locally in chrome.storage.local on your device and are never transmitted anywhere.

AI Features (Optional)

AI features are disabled by default. If you choose to enable them, you pick a provider and supply your own API key. When you send a query, the extension sends a snapshot of your current data view directly to the provider you chose — never through us.

The optional "Analyze with AI" feature lets you upload data to inferenc.es for analysis. This only happens when you explicitly log in, click Analyze, and confirm the upload. Refer to the inferenc.es privacy policy for how they handle data.

Permissions

  • activeTab — Detect your D365 environment URL
  • storage — Save preferences locally
  • D365 host permissions — Send OData queries to your environment
  • AI host permissions (optional) — Only requested if you enable AI features

Children's Privacy

This extension is designed for business use with Dynamics 365 and is not directed at children under 13.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact your organization's IT department.
